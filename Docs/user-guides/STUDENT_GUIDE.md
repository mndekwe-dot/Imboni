# Student Guide

Everything you can do in the Student portal. New here? See
[Getting started](README.md) first.

## Your sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your day at a glance — next classes, recent results, announcements. |
| **Results** | Your marks and grades by subject and term. |
| **Attendance** | Your attendance record and percentage. |
| **Timetable** | Your weekly class schedule. |
| **Assignments** | Work set by your teachers, with due dates. Online quizzes open here. |
| **Activities** | Clubs and activities you can join or are enrolled in. |
| **Discipline** | Any behaviour/conduct records that concern you. |
| **Announcements** | Notices from the school and your teachers. |
| **Messages** | Messages with your teachers. |

## Common tasks

**Check your timetable for today**
Open **Timetable**. Today's column is highlighted. On a phone, use the day tabs
to switch days.

**See a new result**
When a teacher publishes marks you'll get a notification. Open **Results** and
choose the subject/term to see details.

**Do an online quiz**
Open **Assignments**, find the quiz, and select it. Answer the questions and
submit before the due date. After it's graded you can open **Review** to see the
correct answers.

**Join an activity**
Open **Activities**, find one with space, and request to join.

**Message a teacher**
Open **Messages**, start a conversation, choose the teacher, and send.

## Tips
- Turn on **notifications** so you don't miss results or announcements.
- Install Imboni on your phone (see [Getting started](README.md)) for quick access.
