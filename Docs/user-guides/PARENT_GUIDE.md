# Parent / Guardian Guide

Follow your child's school life in the Parent portal. New here? See
[Getting started](README.md) first — including how to open your invitation and
set a password.

## Your sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | A summary across your children — recent results, attendance, notices. |
| **My Children** | Each child's profile and a live view of their day. |
| **Results** | Your child's marks and grades by term. |
| **Attendance** | Attendance record and percentage. |
| **Behaviour** | Conduct and any incidents involving your child. |
| **Announcements** | Notices from the school. |
| **Messages** | Contact the school's staff. |

## Common tasks

**See how my child is doing**
Open **Dashboard** for a quick overview, or **My Children** to focus on one child
and see their live schedule.

**Check results / a report**
Open **Results**, pick the child and term. When new results are published you'll
get a notification (and, if enabled, an email).

**Check attendance**
Open **Attendance** to see the record and overall percentage. You'll be notified
about significant absences.

**Follow up on behaviour or health**
Behaviour records appear under **Behaviour**. For serious incidents or health
matters, the school will notify you; you can reply via **Messages**.

**Message the school**
Open **Messages** and start a conversation with the relevant staff member.

## If you have more than one child
All your children appear together on the **Dashboard** and under **My Children** —
switch between them there.

## Tips
- Keep your **email and phone number** up to date (in your profile) so school
  notifications reach you.
- Turn on **notifications** for results, attendance alerts, and announcements.
