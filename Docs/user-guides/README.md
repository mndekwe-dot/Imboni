## Getting started (everyone)

### Signing in
1. Open Imboni in your browser (your school will give you the web address).
2. Choose your portal from the home page (Student, Teacher, Parent, etc.), or go
   straight to your portal's sign-in page.
3. Enter the **email and password** the school issued you and select **Sign in**.

> **First time here?** You'll receive an **invitation link** by email or SMS from
> the school. Open it and set your password to create your account.

> **Forgot your password?** Password resets are handled by the school office —
> use the contact shown on the sign-in page.

### Two-factor authentication (2FA)
For extra security (recommended for all staff), you can turn on 2FA:
1. Go to **your profile → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Select **Enable**, scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google
   Authenticator, Authy…), and enter the 6-digit code to confirm.
3. **Save the backup codes** somewhere safe — each one lets you sign in once if
   you lose your phone.
After this, signing in asks for a code from your app as a second step.

### Finding your way around
- **Left sidebar** — switch between the sections of your portal. On a phone, tap
  the **menu** button to open it.
- **Notification bell** (top-right) — new activity relevant to you; select one to
  jump to it.
- **Your avatar** (top-right) — opens your **profile & settings** (name, photo,
  password, 2FA).

### Messages
Most portals have a **Messages** section for staying in touch. Messaging follows
the school's policy about who can contact whom (for example, parents message
staff rather than each other).

### Using Imboni offline / installing it
Imboni works as an **installable app**. In a supported browser, choose
**Install** (or "Add to Home screen") to keep it on your device. Some actions —
like a teacher marking attendance — keep working **offline** and sync
automatically when you're back online.

### Getting help
Contact your **school office / administrator** for account problems, or the
contact details shown on your sign-in page.
