# Teacher Guide

Your classes, attendance, results, and assignments in the Teacher portal. New
here? See [Getting started](README.md) first.

## Your sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Today's classes, pending tasks, quick reminders. |
| **Classes** | The classes you teach and their students. |
| **Attendance** | Mark daily attendance for your classes. |
| **Results** | Enter and submit marks for your subjects. |
| **Assignments** | Create assignments and online quizzes; review submissions. |
| **Timetable** | Your teaching schedule. |
| **Students** | Look up students you teach. |
| **Announcements** | Post notices to your classes. |
| **Messages** | Message students, parents, and colleagues (per school policy). |

## Common tasks

**Mark attendance**
Open **Attendance**, choose the class, mark each student present/absent/late, and
save. This **works offline** — if the connection drops, your marks are saved on
the device and sync automatically when you're back online.

**Enter results**
Open **Results**, choose the class, subject, and term, enter the marks, and
**submit**. Submitted results go to the Director of Studies for approval before
they're visible to students and parents.

**Set an assignment or quiz**
Open **Assignments → New**. For a paper assignment, add the title, instructions,
and due date. For an online quiz, add questions (multiple choice, true/false,
short answer…), set the time limit, and publish. Track submissions and grades in
the same place.

**Post an announcement**
Open **Announcements → New**, write it, choose the target class(es), and publish.

**Message a parent or student**
Open **Messages**, start a conversation, and choose the recipient.

## Tips
- Mark attendance even without signal — it syncs later (look for the offline
  indicator).
- Use **Quick Reminders** and **Pending Tasks** on the Dashboard to stay on top
  of the day.
