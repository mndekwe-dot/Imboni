# Matron Guide

Student health, welfare, and boarding care. New here? See
[Getting started](README.md) first.

## Your sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Health and welfare overview — who's in the sick bay, today's medication, alerts. |
| **Health** | Sick-bay records: admit, observe, and discharge students; medication schedules and logs. |
| **Incidents** | Health/welfare incidents; serious ones notify parents. |
| **Students** | Student welfare profiles (including health notes). |
| **Schedule** | Boarding/daily schedule for the students in your care. |
| **Parent Communication** | Contact and log communication with parents. |
| **Messages** | Message staff and parents. |

## Common tasks

**Admit a student to the sick bay**
Open **Health → new record**, choose the student, record the reason, and set the
status (in sick bay / under observation). Discharge them when they're cleared.

**Give and log medication**
In **Health**, a student's medication schedule shows what's due. Record each dose
in the medication log so there's a clear history.

**Record a health incident**
Open **Incidents → new**. For serious or critical incidents, parents are notified
automatically; you can add follow-up notes.

**Contact a parent**
Use **Parent Communication** to reach a parent and log what was discussed, or
**Messages** for a direct conversation.

**Check who needs attention today**
The **Dashboard** shows current sick-bay occupants, medications due, and recent
incidents.

## Note on privacy
Health information is sensitive. Only staff whose role needs it can see these
records, and access is logged. See the school's privacy policy.
