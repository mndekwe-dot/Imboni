# Discipline / Boarding Guide

Student behaviour, boarding management, and student life. New here? See
[Getting started](README.md) first.

## Your sections

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Overview — recent incidents, boarding status, things to follow up. |
| **Students** | Student profiles with behaviour history. |
| **Student Life** | Day-to-day student life management. |
| **Boarding** | Dormitory assignments and boarding details. |
| **Timetable** | The extracurricular / boarding schedule. |
| **Staff** | Discipline/boarding staff. |
| **Student Leaders** | Prefects and student leadership. |
| **Announcements / Messages** | Communicate with students and staff. |

## Common tasks

**Record a behaviour incident**
Open **Students** (or the incident list), choose the student, and add a report —
type, severity, what happened, and any follow-up required. Serious incidents can
notify parents.

**Manage dormitories**
Open **Boarding** to see and adjust dormitory/room assignments and boarding
status.

**Adjust the extracurricular timetable**
Open **Timetable** to edit the activities/boarding schedule.

**Track follow-ups**
Incidents needing follow-up are flagged — filter for pending ones and mark them
complete when resolved.

**Manage student leaders**
Open **Student Leaders** to assign or update prefect roles.

## Tips
- Keep incident records factual and complete — they form the student's history
  and access is logged.
- Use filters (by type, status, or student) to find records quickly.
